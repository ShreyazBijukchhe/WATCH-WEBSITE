We are very familiar to the word "technology" in this digital revolution age.Technology has been part of our life since primitive ages and overtime scientific knowledge expanded enhancing advanced technologies. Technology simply refers to the technologies or tools or ideas which has made our day to day life easier. It has affected in every field of our life. Everything around us like pen, laptops and many more they all are technologies and innovations.Technology plays important roles on innovations and creativity. Technology has made vast changes in our socities, cultures and economics.

In communication field also it has made us easier connect to other people from different parts of the world, in short it has made our whole world smaller like a global village. In many fields, such as information technology, health, and AI, technology has made life more convenient and easier. We can get access to information and news from around the world through means of internet and medias. Different machines like MRI machines, thermometers, stethoscope, and many instruments helps in saving lives of people improving diagnosis. Human enhanced surgical advancements through many improved medical devices. Every energy sources either renewable or non- renewable they all are utilized by means of technologies by the humans. For example solar energy is converted into solar power by means of solar panel. There are many others like hydropower, batteries, LED light. AI technologies has made our day to day work easier and more efficient.AI has made vast differences in automation, healthcare, education. Complex problems are easily solved by the AI. Technology makes people easier for innovating new things more conveniently. Hence, the technology has improved our lives and saved our time.

Though it is has impacted positively in our lives, it also has several drawbacks. Throughout use of heavy machines and technologies has greatly impacted in the environment and ecosystem. Technologies has caused different pollutions and degrade the natural resources. Uncontrolled use of it may cause the extinct in species. Many industries and companies now use robots and AI's to do the task. This has cause growth in unemployment rate. In April 2024, approximately 800 job cuts in the U.S. were attributed directly to AI advancements, marking the highest monthly total since May 2023. Another thing is that as many people are globally connected using internet, it has given birth to cyber crime. Some people may not have the access of new technologies which has resulted them untouched about current affairs. And those who have access to electrical devices like laptops, mobiles they are facing eye problems due to continuous staring at it. Many students rely on AI to solve the problem do the HomeWorks which has made their minds dull. Real life communication is neglected these days as we can reach people just by sitting in our house. This has affected our culture as well. But obviously we can control it by using them wisely and properly.
